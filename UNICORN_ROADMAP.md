## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk: Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL)
**Mitigation:** 
- Clearly state AI provides information, not legal advice
- Partner with licensed attorneys for review
- Comply with all state bar regulations

### Risk: AI Errors
**Mitigation:**
- Human review for high-stakes cases
- Continuous monitoring and improvement
- Insurance for errors and omissions

### Risk: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- Move fast, build moat with data and brand
- Patent AI legal tech innovations
- Lock in customers with great experience

### Risk: Regulatory Changes
**Mitigation:**
- Lobby for AI-friendly legal tech regulations
- Adapt quickly to new rules
- Diversify across states and specializations
